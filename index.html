🎓 Exam Prep

NPSE Revision Centre

For Class 5 & 6 students preparing for the National Primary School Examination. Practice papers, timed tests and AI explanations.

📋
What is the NPSE?

The National Primary School Examination is taken by Class 6 pupils in Sierra Leone. It determines entry into secondary school. Preparation through past papers is key to success.

⏱️
Timed Practice Mode
Simulate real exam conditions
📋 What is in General Paper?
General Paper (NPSE 06) is one exam combining 5 subjects — Science · Agricultural Science · Home Economics · Physical & Health Education · Social Studies. All 50 questions come in one paper. Revise all five areas to score well!

Realistic NPSE-style questions with full explanations — available now while real papers are being uploaded.

Last updated: May 2026 · Wisdom Academy International, #37 Kossoh Town, New London, Sierra Leone
1. Who We Are

Wisdom Academy International ("we", "us", "our") operates the WAI Digital Learning Platform. We are a daycare, nursery and primary school located at #37 Kossoh Town, New London, Freetown, Sierra Leone. Contact: wisdom17academy@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our platform: your name and email address when you register or download materials; your WAI registration number (for school members); your country of residence; usage data such as which lessons and features you access. We do not collect payment information directly — this will be handled by secure third-party processors when subscriptions launch.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide you with access to lessons, games and educational content; send you updates about new content and platform features; verify your WAI membership status; improve our platform based on usage patterns. We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We never display advertisements.

4. Children\'s Privacy

This platform serves children aged 2-11. We take children's privacy extremely seriously. Child accounts must be created and managed by a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Children do not communicate publicly on this platform. Student photos are displayed only with confirmed parental consent.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use trusted third-party services (Supabase) for data storage, which complies with international data protection standards. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing wisdom17academy@gmail.com

6. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal information we hold about you; correct inaccurate information; request deletion of your data; withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at wisdom17academy@gmail.com

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the platform to function — such as keeping you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes by email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
